01 · My Story

"Fatty liver" sounded manageable. It wasn't.

I didn't feel sick, that's the terrifying part. By the time I had answers, my condition had progressed to liver fibrosis stage 3, and I was facing advanced scarring, an uncertain future, and a deep sense of isolation.

I turned that fear into a mission: to learn everything, change how I live, and stand up so the next person catches it sooner.

02 · Understanding MASH

A silent condition, explained in plain language.

MASH, metabolic dysfunction-associated steatohepatitis, is one of the fastest-growing liver conditions in the world, and most people who have it don't know yet.

What it is

Fat in the liver, plus inflammation and cell damage.

Why it matters

Left unchecked it can progress, caught early, the liver can heal.
