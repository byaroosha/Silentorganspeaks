Mediterranean Lentil & Herb Bowl

High fiberPlant-based30 minutes

A warm, nourishing bowl built on protein- and fiber-rich lentils, brightened with lemon, good olive oil, and a whole garden of fresh herbs. It keeps blood sugar steady and keeps you satisfied.

You'll need

  • 1 cup cooked green or brown lentils
  • 2 cups mixed greens & herbs
  • Cherry tomatoes & cucumber
  • Extra-virgin olive oil + lemon
  • Toasted walnuts, salt & pepper

Method

  • 1. Toss lentils with olive oil & lemon.
  • 2. Fold in chopped herbs and greens.
  • 3. Add tomatoes and cucumber.
  • 4. Top with walnuts; season and serve.

Swap this featured recipe for your own signature dish anytime.
